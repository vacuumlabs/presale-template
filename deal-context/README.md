# deal-context/

Sales and account management context: who the client is, who decides what, what they're trying to achieve, how they feel about us as delivery progresses.

## Typical wiki pages

- `stakeholder-map.md` — overall picture of who's involved
- `stakeholders/<name>.md` — one page per named stakeholder (role, influence, sentiment, what they want, what they fear)
- `client-motivation.md` — why they're buying, what success looks like for them
- `client-sentiment.md` — rolling journal of sentiment shifts (from BD calls, steering committees, retrospectives)
- `competitive-context.md` — who else they considered or still could turn to
- `account-strategy.md` — our plan for the relationship beyond this engagement
