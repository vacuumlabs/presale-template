# product-management/

Problem framing, product strategy, user and market research, personas, PRDs, user journeys, UX and product flows, feature maps, competitive analysis, MVP scope, product phasing, and roadmap.

## Typical wiki pages

### Problem definition
- `problem-framing.md` — problem space exploration, context, constraints, and how the problem was discovered
- `problem-statement.md` — the sharpest, most actionable version of the problem we're solving

### Strategy & business
- `product-strategy.md` — product vision, strategic goals, and positioning
- `value-proposition.md` — value proposition per target segment
- `business-case.md` — business justification, ROI framing, and success metrics

### Research
- `user-research.md` — synthesised findings from interviews, surveys, and observations
- `pain-points.md` — synthesised user pain points across personas and research sessions
- `market-research.md` — market size, trends, opportunities, TAM/SAM/SOM
- `business-research.md` — domain and industry context, regulatory landscape, vocabulary
- `user-testing.md` — usability findings, test results, and iteration notes
- `product-analysis.md` — analysis of the current or incumbent product (if applicable)
- `benchmarking.md` — benchmarking against industry standards, metrics, or best practices
- `competitive-analysis.md` — competitive landscape overview, positioning map
- `competitors/<name>.md` — one page per competitor (strengths, weaknesses, signals observed)

### Users
- `personas/<name>.md` — one page per persona. When the canonical source is a polished PO-authored file under `team-inputs/`, the wiki page references it via `canonical_source:` metadata rather than duplicating

### Product definition
- `prd/<feature-or-epic>.md` — product requirements document per feature or epic
- `use-cases.md` — use case definitions (actors, preconditions, main flow, alternatives)
- `journeys/<name>.md` — user journey maps (stages, actions, emotions, pain points)
- `service-blueprint.md` — service blueprint (frontstage, backstage, support processes)
- `ux-flows/<name>.md` — UX flows and wireframe narratives
- `product-flows/<name>.md` — end-to-end product flows across systems and actors
- `feature-map.md` — structural map of all features, grouped by area or epic
- `feature-list.md` — flat catalogue of features with status, priority, and owning persona
- `feature-prioritisation.md` — prioritisation framework (e.g. RICE, MoSCoW) and output

### Scope & roadmap
- `mvp-scope.md` — what is in and out of MVP, rationale for each call
- `product-scope.md` — full product scope definition across all phases
- `product-phasing.md` — how the product rolls out across phases (what ships when and why)
- `product-roadmap.md` — timeline-linked roadmap tied to milestones and resourcing
