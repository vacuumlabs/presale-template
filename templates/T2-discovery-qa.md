# T2 — Discovery Q&A Document

_Used during the discovery phase (Stage 3). Can be sent to the client in advance of a workshop, filled in async, or used as a structured agenda for the discovery call. Add answers during and after calls — rough notes are fine._

**Usage:** Copy this file to `team-inputs/T2-discovery-qa.md` and fill it in. Run `/ingest team-inputs/T2-discovery-qa.md` after each call, even with partial answers.

---

## Section 1 — Business Context

1. What is the primary business objective behind this initiative? What changes in your business when this project succeeds?
2. What is driving the timing — why now?
3. Who are the key decision-makers for this project? Who signs off on the contract?
4. What does the board / executive team care about most in this engagement?
5. Have you run a project of this scope before? What happened?

---

## Section 2 — Current State

6. Walk us through your current system landscape: what platforms are you running, how old are they, and what are their main limitations?
7. Which of your current systems are in scope for this project (to be replaced, integrated with, or extended)?
8. What do your operations and back-office teams currently spend the most time on manually?
9. What reporting and data access do you currently have? What would you like to have?

---

## Section 3 — Products and Users

10. List the products / services you currently offer. For each: who uses it, how it works at a high level, and the main friction point.
11. Are there products or services you want to launch as part of this initiative that you don't currently offer?
12. Who are your end users? Rough volumes: DAU/MAU, transaction volumes, loan book size, etc.

---

## Section 4 — Technical Landscape

13. What is your current core banking system (if applicable)? Any preferences or constraints on what replaces it?
14. What third-party systems must this project integrate with?
15. Do you have existing API documentation or a system architecture diagram you can share?
16. What are your non-functional requirements? (uptime SLA, transaction throughput, data residency, disaster recovery)
17. What is your current tech team structure? Will Vacuumlabs be working alongside an internal team, or owning the full build?

---

## Section 5 — Constraints

18. What budget has been approved for this initiative?
19. Are there any regulatory or compliance requirements that directly constrain the architecture or timeline?
20. Are there any technology or vendor constraints we should know about?
21. What is the latest acceptable go-live date for the first phase? What is the target?

---

## Section 6 — Operating Model & Success

_These questions feed the Business Architecture output (P3e). Skip if this is a T1 engagement._

22. Which internal teams will be most affected by this project? What does their day look like today, and how would it change?
23. Are there roles or capabilities the client will need to build internally to run the new system? (e.g. a product configuration team, a new ops process)
24. What would "a year after go-live" look like if this project succeeded? What would your team be doing differently?
25. What metrics or KPIs would you use to judge whether this project delivered what it promised?
